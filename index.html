Have you ever left home and forgot the lights off? Or maybe decided to come back home earlier and realized you forgot to open the windows in order to air the room. Or decided to heat up the room, so that when you'll arrive it will feel pleasant and warm. What about the thieves or burglars?

How would you feel like being able to control and monitor your house in all these situations by using just your smartphone or tablet?

With the new SmartHome application available both on tablet and smartphones you could easily control your house while you're not there:

  • Turn on / off the lights
  • Open / Close windows
  • Watch all the rooms live
  • Turn on / off the heat

The application is available both on Microsoft's MarketStore and on iOS and Android's stores.
